    Published Paper

    Analysis and compression of ECG signal using DDC methods along with feature extraction
    project focuses on addressing challenges related to ambulatory monitoring of ECG signals, such as power consumption, storage, and transmission. It proposes an efficient ECG signal compression algorithm using combinations of Turning Point (TP) and AZTEC Direct Data compression methods. The evaluation is based on performance parameters like Average R-R Values, Compression Ratio (CR), and Percentage Mean Square Difference (PRD). A comparative analysis with TP, AZTEC, and three proposed methods shows that the proposed methodologies outperform previous work. The implementation and simulation are conducted in LabVIEW 2018.
